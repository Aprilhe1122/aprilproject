Discover Classical Chinese Elegance

Founded in 1998 in the culturally vibrant city of Guangzhou, Guangdong, Ming Furniture brings the elegance of Ming and Qing dynasty furnishings to modern homes.

Our exquisite collection reflects the artistry and heritage of two of China's most prosperous dynasties. With every piece, we bring to life over 5000 years of cultural tradition.

Crafted with precision and care, our furniture is made from premium materials including huanghuali, chicken-wing wood, oak, rosewood, and merbau.

Explore a rich variety of offerings such as canopy beds, round-back armchairs, tea tables, wardrobes, window lattices, jewelry boxes, brass locks, and more.

Our three-story showroom houses over 10,000 unique pieces, each ready to find a new home.
