Explore Our Gallery

Our collection features over 10,000 authentic pieces that reflect the grandeur of Ming and Qing era design.

Each item in our showroom is a testament to traditional Chinese aesthetics and craftsmanship. From elegant tea tables to ornate wardrobes, our gallery is a visual journey through history.

Whether you're an interior designer, a collector, or simply an admirer of heritage art, you'll find inspiration in our diverse offerings.

Our three-floor exhibition space showcases classic pieces like the Luohan bed, round-back armchairs, and delicately carved window panels.
