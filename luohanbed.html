Luohan Bed - Huanghuali

The Huanghuali Luohan bed is a traditional high-end piece of Chinese furniture, known for its luxurious appearance, elegant curves, and long-lasting quality.

Huanghuali wood is rare and valuable, prized for its beautiful grain and color. The Luohan bed is a special type of bed inspired by the serene seated posture of enlightened Buddhist figures known as Luohans.

Each piece is handcrafted with unique characteristics and fine details. Artisans use traditional tools and techniques to meticulously carve every corner and angle, enhancing the beauty and prestige of the furniture.
