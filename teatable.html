Tea Table

A harmonious blend of nature and craftsmanship, this tea table is carved from authentic Indonesian pinewood. Its vivid annual rings tell the story of time, with a striking contrast between spring’s softness and autumn’s density.

Graceful resin channels flow through its surface like nature’s own brushstrokes, revealing the wood’s organic beauty. The sapwood glows with a warm yellow-white hue, while the heartwood radiates deep reddish-browns, maturing into a burnished orange-red as it ages — a transformation as poetic as tea itself.

This table is not just a piece of furniture — it is a stage for quiet reflection, a partner in ritual, and a celebration of natural elegance.
