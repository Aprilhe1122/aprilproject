Jewelry Box

This exquisite jewelry box is handcrafted from Indian small-leaf Zitan (red sandalwood), a timber long revered as the "Wood of Emperors." Its remarkably dense grain, shifting hues from rich red-brown to deep violet-black, and silky, intricate texture make it a material unlike any other.

Zitan grows with an almost meditative slowness — a single growth ring every five years, requiring over 800 years to mature. It is the hardest of all woods, and its rarity only adds to its legendary status.

What sets this piece apart is its joinery craftsmanship. Built entirely with traditional Chinese mortise-and-tenon joints, it contains no nails, no screws — only timeless precision. This ancient technique is the soul of classical Chinese furniture, allowing the box to endure for generations while preserving a sense of elegance and spiritual integrity.

This jewelry box is not just a container — it is a treasure in itself, holding not only your adornments but also centuries of artistry and heritage.
