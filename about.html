Our Story

Ming Furniture was founded in 1998 in the culturally rich city of Guangzhou, Guangdong Province. Our company specializes in classical Chinese furniture inspired by the elegance and simplicity of the Ming and Qing dynasties.

With a deep respect for heritage and craftsmanship, we are committed to preserving traditional Chinese aesthetics and passing on the beauty of ancient design to future generations.

Our Founder
